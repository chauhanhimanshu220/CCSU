## Design Direction

Portal ko intentionally institutional but modern feel dene ke liye design ko teen principles par shape kiya gaya:

### 1. Formal but approachable

- serif headings give an academic and official tone
- soft rounded containers reduce portal fatigue
- muted explanatory copy keeps dense forms readable

### 2. Guided progression

- left-side stepper shows the full recruitment flow
- active and completed steps are visually distinct
- modals are used for credential reveal and final submission confirmation

### 3. Warm visual identity

- cream background
- deep green primary actions
- amber secondary actions
- subtle gradients and glass panels for depth

## Core UI Components

- login hero panel
- credential popup
- wizard stepper
- section cards
- upload status cards
- dashboard progress cards
- payment pending popup

## Responsive Strategy

- two-column layouts collapse to one column on smaller screens
- form grids become stacked
- buttons wrap instead of overflowing
- modal content stays centered with padding
